# RiskLedger Compliance Platform

A next-generation compliance engine that modernizes KYC/KYB onboarding and AML decisioning with AI-assisted workflows, a governed data layer, and a privacy engine. RiskLedger ingests verification requests, consolidates signals from numerous sources (internal datasets, watchlists, synthetic adverse media), reconciles identities, computes risk scores, and produces explainable, auditable reports—while minimizing exposure of sensitive personal information (PII).

## Why this project

Traditional compliance operations rely on fragmented tools and manual consolidation, which leads to:

- Slow onboarding
- Inconsistent decisions
- Difficult audits
- Increased privacy exposure

RiskLedger tackles these challenges by embracing:

- **Decision intelligence** – explainable scoring grounded in evidence
- **Privacy-by-design** – tokenization, masking, and controlled access
- **Full traceability** – append-only audit events and replay capability

## Key Capabilities

### Case Management & Workflow

- Create and track cases
- Assign users, add notes, escalate issues
- Status management and audit trail

### Data Ingestion & Curation

- RAW → CURATED normalization pipeline
- Data quality scoring at each step
- Flexible connectors for external and internal sources

### Privacy Engine

- Masking, tokenization, and anonymization
- Controlled re‑identification (supervisor-only, justified, time-bounded)
- Privacy-first design across every component

### Watchlist Screening

- Sanctions / PEP list checks
- Match confidence scoring and reporting

### Adverse Media NLP

- PII scrubbing from unstructured sources
- Event extraction with severity scoring
- Supports synthetic adverse media ingestion

### ML Decision Support

- **Entity Resolution** – identity matching and deduplication
- **Anomaly Detection** – spotting suspicious reuse patterns
- **Risk Scoring** – 0–100 score with categories and reason codes

### Explainable Reporting

- Generate redacted, human-readable reports
- Ground decisions in evidence for auditors and regulators

### Audit & Replay Foundation

- Append-only audit events for full history
- Evidence snapshots and export-ready traceability
- Enables replay of decisioning workflows for compliance
